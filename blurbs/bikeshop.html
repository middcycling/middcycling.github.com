Campus Bike Shop

The Middlebury on-campus bike shop is back and with expanded coverage and doubled hours, they are working harder than ever to get your bike ready to ride! The shop is located in the basement of the Adironcack House - the door faces Ross Commons and has an old bicycle wheel hanging outside. Swing by on Tues, Wed, Thurs: 7p - 10p or Fri, Sat: 5p - 8p to work with experienced mechanics and get your bike in good shape for the road or trail. For more information, visit their site here.
